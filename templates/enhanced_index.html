Advanced Analytics Features
Risk Heatmap

Visual representation of risk levels over time, showing monthly aggregation of BRI values with color-coded risk categories. Helps identify patterns and seasonal trends in market behavioral risk.

Volatility Clustering

Advanced machine learning analysis that groups market periods into low, moderate, and high volatility clusters. Uses K-means clustering on volatility features to identify distinct market regimes.

Early Warning System

Dynamic threshold-based system that detects potential risk spikes before they occur. Uses rolling percentiles to identify unusual BRI movements and provides early alerts for risk management.

Forecasting & Confidence Intervals

Forecasting & Confidence Intervals

Predictive analytics with confidence intervals for BRI forecasting. Provides 7-day ahead predictions with uncertainty quantification to support risk management decisions.
